## 1. Overview
Tripple 7 Movie Empire is a startup company that plans to compete in the movie industry by launching a new movie studio. This project provides a data-driven analysis of historical movie performance to help stakeholders understand industry trends and make informed decisions on movie production, release timing, and budget allocation.


## 2. Business Problem
Tripple Seven plans to launch a new movie studio but lacks experience in the film industry. The company does not know which types of movies perform best at the box office or what factors contribute to financial success.

Without proper analysis, investing in movie production could lead to financial losses. This project uses historical movie data to identify profitable trends and reduce investment risk.
